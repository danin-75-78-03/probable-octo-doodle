National Anti-Ragging Day: August 12

Designated by the NMC, this day serves as a powerful reminder of our collective responsibility to create a safe and nurturing environment for all students. It's a day for introspection, awareness, and renewing our commitment to a zero-tolerance policy towards ragging.

Promoting Empathy & Respect

Fostering a culture where senior students act as mentors, not tormentors, and where mutual respect is the foundation of all interactions.

Awareness of Consequences

Educating every student about the severe legal and academic repercussions of ragging, ensuring no one can claim ignorance.

Encouraging Bystander Intervention

Empowering students to safely speak up and intervene when they witness an act of ragging, breaking the cycle of silence.
